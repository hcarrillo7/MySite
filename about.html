About Me

Just a little about myself...

I'm a 22 year old Computer Science graduate from CSUSM. I was born and raised in San Diego and enjoy everything it has to offer. When I'm not in front of a computer screen I like to remain active, whether it's playing soccer, going on a mountain bike ride or simply hanging out with friends. My interest for technology sparked at a very young age, but it wasn't until after my first programming course when I knew I had found what I wanted to do for the rest of my life. I always enjoy a new challenge and the learning that comes with it. I thrive in a team environment and becoming a developer simply seems like the perfect fit.
