Projects

Cougar Post

Cougar Post was the result of a group project from my CIS 444 Web Development Course at CSUSM. Our goal was to find a solution for the clutter of bulletins found around our campus, and we came up with Cougar Post. Cougar Post is a virtual solution for bulletins where students, faculty and local business can easily access the campus's community.

Adventures of Val and Tia

Adventures of Val and Tia was a very fun project for my CS 485 Game Programming course. Myself, along with my two other partners, Jessica Jones and Vincent Phongsamran, had the opportunity to create our own video game. We were responsible for all aspects of the game including: design, animations, functionality and overall gameplay.
