Cougar Post

That One School Project...

Cougar Post is an online bulletin board for the CSUSM community. Working in a team environment, I was responsible for the design and implementation of the site's front end. Our goal was to create a website where users can comfortably access and navigate through posts to find what they are looking for or just as easily post a bulletin themselves. We took a Pinterest style approach to the layout since we felt it was the most effective way of displaying information in a clean and non-cluttered way. A little extra fun was added with the integration of Google+ Authentication, users log in using their school assigned google account and as a result we are given access to their name, picture and email which we use to create a safer and more welcoming site, especially when compared to other sites like Craigslist.

Tools Used: HTML, CSS, Javascript, PHP, AJAX, PHPMyAdmin, Google API, Bootstrap
