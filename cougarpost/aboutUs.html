About Us

Our team goal is to make a website that will help our fellow students communicate in a more efficient manner. We feel there is a need for a website like this on campus to help students connect in ways that have not yet been made possible. Our website, Cougar Post, is the missing piece to the puzzle. Cougar Post is an online bulletin board for CSUSM students and administration alike. Students will use Cougar Post to announce events, join study groups and post items to buy and/or sell. This will eliminate the need for old fashioned bulletin boards that can easily be missed and will increase both visibility and interactions amongst the CSUSM populous. Cougar Post will forever change the way Cougars interact.
